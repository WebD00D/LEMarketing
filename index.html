Our 3 step process

Through trial and error working closely with over 40 clients now, we have a collaborative branding process that outputs quality results that we are proud to provide, and our clients are proud to show off.

Brand Discovery
Understand the business / brand and find the key areas we can help tailor to deliver results.

Plan of Action
Based on the specifics of the project's needs we layout a time-frame of development and delegate each task to the most qualified team member.

Tailor & Deliver
We understand quality testing and valuable results necessary to deliver you the best chances of ROI. We fine tune the details as much as possible to allow the greatest chance of delivery for that bottom line.



1. Brand Discovery

Through our research and brand analysis process we figure out exactly what makes you and your target demographic tick, to allow for the best conversion rates and bottom line results possible.

  • Identify your brand characteristics
  • Create profile of target demographic

Next: Plan of Action



2. Plan of Action

The Laborem design team starts with a conceptual design outlining the foundations of the project logo, web site, or branding material; creates a digitized prototype; then build out a working model.

  • UX/UI Creation
  • Digitalized prototype

Next: Tailor & Deliver



3. Tailor & Deliver

Once the discovery, planning and build-out phases are complete and approved by the client, it is time to refine the material and bring the vision to life.

  • A/B Testing
  • Final Revisions
  • Presentation and Delivery of Assets
  • Training and support
