The booming salon in Richmond, Bombshell, reached out to us to create a logo for their internal classes they hold for their employees, "Bombshell Beauty School".

We worked with their educational instructors to create a logo that reflected their current brand, but something to seperate Bombshell Beauty School as its own unique piece of the pie.

They loved the logo, and are currently working with us now to re-vamp their website.

Brewrocket, a local start up needed a complete system built out for their online homebrewing 101 educational course.

We spent 3 months planning, designing, and building their website and database and even put together an android app for their users to learn on the go.

Brewrocket is continuing to help people learn how to homebrew, and we had a blast working with their team.

A local cigar shop in Midlothian, Virginia not only wanted a stronger online presence, but the ability to sell their inventory online.

Utilizing .NET technologies, we built Winston's Humidor a complete online store, a blog, as well a dashboard to fully manage their inventory.

Winston's Humidor is currently in the process of preparing their inventory, and should expect to be up live late October 2015.

Orinoco Cigars is a cigar manufacturer that was referred to us through our project with Winston's Humidor. We're currently developing a website for them that better communicates their brand.

Each one of their cigars is based off a historical object, or event which we are fully taking advantage of in our design process.

You can expect to see the site live early November.
